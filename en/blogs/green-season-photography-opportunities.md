## Introduction

As a photography blogger, I've traveled to 50+ nature reserves worldwide, photographed countless photos. But April 2025's Serengeti still shocked me.

Many say, rainy season (March-May) not best time for Safari‚Äîmuddy roads, frequent rain, low visibility.

## Trip Highlights

### Day 1: Arrive in Serengeti - Emerald Savanna

**10:00 AM - Arrive in Serengeti**

I chose April Serengeti, rainy season mid-term, savanna already completely green.

My guide was John, Maasai guide worked in Serengeti 12 years, knew rainy season photography locations inside out.

"Welcome to Serengeti," John smiled, "Rainy season Serengeti is photographer's paradise."

**2:00 PM - First Game Drive**

Less than 10 minutes out of camp, I was shocked by sight‚Äîemerald savanna, blue sky, white clouds, plus herds of wildebeest and zebras.

"Rainy season savanna is green," John said, "This is most beautiful color."

I took out camera, started photographing. Emerald savanna, golden sunlight, white clouds, this perfect composition.

**4:00 PM - Wildebeest in Rain**

Suddenly, sky started raining. Not heavy rain, but light rain, like thin veil covering savanna.

"Don't put away camera," John said, "Photos in rain most beautiful."

I continued photographing. Wildebeest in rain, zebras in rain, giraffes in rain, everything became hazy, soft, poetic.

"This is rainy season's charm," John said, "Light soft, colors saturated, suitable for photography."

**7:00 PM - Camp Dinner**

We had dinner at camp. Rain still falling, but very gentle, like lullaby.

"Rainy season nights very quiet," John said, "Few tourists, can hear animal sounds."

### Day 2: Photography Masterclass - Light's Art

**6:00 AM - Morning Light**

John suggested we depart at 6 AM: "Morning light best for photography."

Early morning Serengeti beautiful as painting‚Äîgolden morning light, mist, emerald savanna.

"Look at clouds there," John pointed at sky, "Those are rainy season's unique clouds."

I looked up, giant clouds in sky, like cotton candy, golden sunlight penetrating clouds, falling on savanna.

"This is photographer's favorite light," John said, "Soft, warm, full of drama."

**8:00 AM - Photographing Lions**

We encountered lion pride‚Äî8 lions, including male lion and two females, plus 5 cubs.

"Lions very active in rainy season," John said, "Because food abundant."

"This is rainy season's charm," John said, "Light soft, colors saturated, suitable for photography."

**2:00 PM - Photographing Migratory Birds**

Rainy season is migratory bird season, Serengeti has hundreds of migratory bird species.

We photographed flamingos, storks, herons, eagles... each bird has own unique beauty.

"Rainy season's migratory birds most beautiful," John said, "Because they're in breeding season, feathers most colorful."

**5:00 PM - Sunset's Magnificence**

Evening, we waited for sunset on open savanna. Sun setting, golden light on emerald savanna, distant clouds like burning flames.

"This is rainy season's most spectacular sunset," John said, "Clouds, light, colors, everything perfect."

I pressed shutter, recorded this spectacular moment.

### üéí Packing Recommendations

**Essential Gear**:
- High-end camera + telephoto lens (at least 400mm, 600mm better)
- Tripod (for stable shooting)
- Binoculars (8x42 or 10x42)
- Rain cover (protect equipment)
- Waterproof bag (protect electronic devices)
- Sunscreen and hat
- Lightweight jacket (rainy season cool)
- Power bank (charging in wild)
- Notebook and pen (record observations)

**Optional Items**:
- Recording device (record rain sounds)
- Shade cloth (for stealth shooting)
- Wet wipes
- Mosquito repellent (rainy season needed)

### üí° Tips

1. **Best time**: March-May rainy season, April peak season, savanna greenest, most migratory birds.

2. **Best light**: Morning (6-8am) and evening (5-7pm) light best, suitable for photography.

3. **Photography in rain**: Don't put away camera, photos in rain most beautiful. Use rain cover protect equipment.

4. **Choose professional guide**: Good guide can take you to best shooting angles, predict light changes.

5. **Stay patient**: Rainy season weather changes fast, stay patient, wait for perfect light.

6. **Respect nature**: Don't disturb animals, don't destroy their habitats, don't use flash.

7. **Record everything**: Photograph, video, write diary, these records valuable for photography creation.
