## Zanzibar vs Mafia Island

### Zanzibar

**Advantages**:
- Convenient transportation: 1-hour flight from Arusha, 30-minute flight from Dar es Salaam
- Many accommodation options: From budget to luxury, many choices
- Rich activities: Stone Town, spice farms, dolphins, diving
- History and culture: Rich history and culture

**Disadvantages**:
- Many tourists: Zanzibar is popular, many tourists
- Commercialized: Zanzibar has been commercialized, losing its primitive beauty

**Suitable for**:
- First time in Tanzania
- Like history and culture
- Like lively atmosphere
- Limited budget

### Mafia Island

**Advantages**:
- Few tourists: Mafia Island is niche, few tourists
- Primitive: Mafia Island hasn't been commercialized, maintaining its primitive beauty
- Whale sharks: Mafia Island is Tanzania's best whale shark diving site
- Coral reefs: Mafia Island has rich coral reefs

**Disadvantages**:
- Inconvenient transportation: 3-hour boat from Dar es Salaam, no direct flight
- Few accommodation options: Only mid-range hotels, no luxury hotels
- Limited activities: Only diving and snorkeling, no other activities

**Suitable for**:
- Diving enthusiasts
- Like primitive places
- Like quiet atmosphere
- Sufficient budget

### How to Choose

**Choose Zanzibar if**:
- It's your first time in Tanzania
- You like history and culture
- You like lively atmosphere
- You have limited budget

**Choose Mafia Island if**:
- You're a diving enthusiast
- You like primitive places
- You like quiet atmosphere
- You have sufficient budget

## Domestic Flight Booking Tips

### Book in Advance

Domestic flights need to be booked in advance, especially during peak season (July-October).

"Book 3 months in advance," I always tell my clients, "Domestic flights are very popular."

### Choose the Right Airport

Zanzibar has two airports:
- Zanzibar Airport (ZNZ): International airport, with international flights
- Zanzibar Airport (ZNZ): Domestic airport, only domestic flights

Mafia Island has no airport, needs boat from Dar es Salaam.

### Book the Right Flight

When booking domestic flights, pay attention to:
- Flight time: Choose morning or afternoon flights, avoid midday flights (too hot)
- Airline: Choose experienced airlines like Air Tanzania, Precision Air
- Flight price: Booking in advance can save 30-50% of cost
- Flight cancellation: Domestic flights often cancel, booking in advance can avoid

### Dealing with Flight Cancellations

If a flight is cancelled, don't panic, you have options:
- Reschedule: Reschedule to next flight
- Refund: Refund and choose other transportation
- Accommodation: If overnight stay is needed, book accommodation
