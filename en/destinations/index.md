# 14 Destinations, 14 Faces of Africa

From the endless plains of the Serengeti to the glacier-capped peaks of Kilimanjaro; from the white sands of Lake Tanganyika to the spice markets of Zanzibarâ€”the diversity of Tanzania is beyond imagination.

Here lies the world's most spectacular wildlife migration, Africa's highest peak, the earliest footprints of humanity, and 400 years of Swahili culture. Each of these 14 destinations is worthy of its own journey, and each can change your perception of Africa.

**How to Choose?** First-time visitors should start with the Northern Circuit; veteran players can explore the South and West; trekking enthusiasts can challenge Kilimanjaro; and vacationers can head to Zanzibar.
