The heart of Mafia Island is **Chole Bay Marine Park**, featuring:

*   **Complete Coral Reef Ecosystem**: Over 50 coral species and 460 tropical fish species.

*   **Marine Big Five**: Beyond whale sharks, you can see dolphins, hawksbill turtles, green turtles, and occasionally even dugongs (relatives of manatees).
