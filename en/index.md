**A:** If you want to see the Great Migration, **July to October** is peak season. For the calving season (thousands of babies being born!), **January to March** is magical.

**A:** Tanzania is one of the safest countries in Africa. Our professional guides and 4x4 vehicles are your "mobile fortresses." We ensure you are close to nature but far from danger.
