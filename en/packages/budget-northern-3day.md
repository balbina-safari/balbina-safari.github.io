## Budget-Friendly Safari Introduction

Designed for travelers with limited budgets who are eager to experience Tanzania's wildlife. This 3-day trip concentrates the essence of the northern circuit with budget-friendly camp/lodge accommodation, significantly reducing costs through group sharing. Despite the affordable price, the quality of the safari is not compromised!

**Perfect for**: Backpackers, students, first-time safari-goers, budget travelers

---

## Detailed Itinerary

### Day 1: Arusha ‚Üí Tarangire

**Accommodation**: Fanaka Campsite or similar (public bathroom tented camp)
**Transport**: [Safari 4x4]({{ site.baseurl }}/transportations/safari-4x4) (shared group, max 7 people)
**Meals**: [Lunch Box]({{ site.baseurl }}/services/lunch-box) + Camp dinner

**Itinerary**:
- 08:00 Pickup from Arusha hotel/backpacker hostel
- 10:00 Arrive at [Tarangire]({{ site.baseurl }}/destinations/tarangire)
- Full-day game drive: elephants, baobab trees, kopjes
- 18:00 Return to camp
- 19:00 Camp dinner (local specialties)

### Day 2: Tarangire ‚Üí Ngorongoro

**Accommodation**: Simba Campsite (public campsite at crater rim)
**Transport**: Safari 4x4
**Meals**: Camp breakfast + lunch box + camp dinner

**Itinerary**:
- 06:30 Depart after breakfast
- Brief game drive through [Lake Manyara]({{ site.baseurl }}/destinations/lake-manyara)
  - Look for tree-climbing lions
  - Watch flamingos
- Afternoon arrive at Ngorongoro Conservation Area
- Evening hike to crater rim viewpoint for sunset

### Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater ‚Üí Arusha

**Transport**: Safari 4x4
**Meals**: Camp breakfast + lunch box

**Itinerary**:
- 06:00 Breakfast (packed)
- 06:30 Descend into [Ngorongoro Crater]({{ site.baseurl }}/destinations/ngorongoro)
  - 5-6 hours crater game drive
  - Search for black rhinos, lion prides, hippos
  - Flamingo lakes
- 13:00 Leave crater, return to Arusha
- 17:00 Arrive in Arusha (transfer to hotel/bus station)
