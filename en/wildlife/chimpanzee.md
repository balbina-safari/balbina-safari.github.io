## Overview

Humanity's closest relatives. Can manufacture and use tools, possess cultural transmission. Jane Goodall's research at Gombe Stream changed human understanding of animal intelligence. Mahale Mountains and Gombe Stream are Tanzania's only chimpanzee habitats.
