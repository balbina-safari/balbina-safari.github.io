## Overview

Very photogenic beautiful monkey. Long white 'cape' and tail fur like a cloak. Mainly eats leaves, specialized multi-chambered stomach can digest cellulose. Name 'Colobus' comes from Greek, meaning 'mutilated', because they lack thumbs.
